Memories

It was 14th March,a random Saturday eve to everyone out there but ask us what the day was!!! It was our Fresher's Day - AMORTENTIA 2020. The same day with hearts full of excitement and energy we heard colleges gonna remain shut for next few days and from then it passed almost 15 months we met each other, bunked classes together, laughed together in the canteen, worn the uniform. We didn't know that Goodbye on our Fresher's Eve was going to be this tough. We miss each other and all the college memories so much that it can't be explained and eagerly waiting to get back those days. So,to us AMORTENTIA is our best and till now the last and most fresh memory of college life we cherish everyday.This sector is just a small glimpse of our Most Fresh and delightful College Life.
